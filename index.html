The Most Epic Nerby Bar Dice Game

Rules:

  • Two players face off against each other.
  • Standard bar dice rules apply
  • Five dice will be rolled up to three times to get the highest score.
  • To score one or more of the five dice must be a '1'.
  • The score is determined by the number of '1's rolled plus the highest instance of another rolled dice
  • For example if for 5 six-sided dice you rolled a 1,1,3,3,4 your score will be 43 (2 '1's + 2 '3's equals 4 with the number your chose being the second digit).
  • Another example would be 1,1,2,4,6 which would give you a score of 36
  • In between rolls you are given the option to keep whichever dice you want from your roll and re-roll the rest of the dice.
  • Six round will be played using 4-sided die in the first round, then 6-sided, 8-sided, 10-sided, 12-sided and 20-sided
  • For the 4-sided and 6-sided round the numbers on the dice themselves will be used to determined the score.
  • For the 8,10,12,20 sided die the remainder when divided by a smaller number will be used to keep the score.
  • For the 8-sided die the divisor will be 4. For the 12-sided die the divisor will be 6. For the other two the divisor will be 5.
  • For example for the 12 sided die rolling a 7 will be the same as rolling as 1 as 7 % 6 = 1
  • When the remainder is 0 the divisor will be the score (for the 12-sided die rolling a 12 is the same a rolling a 6).
  • The dice roll will be visible in the console.
  • The score will be determined internally and the winner will be announced at the end of all rounds
